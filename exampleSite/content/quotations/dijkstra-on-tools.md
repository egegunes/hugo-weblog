quote: |
  The tools we use have a profound (and devious!) influence on our thinking habits, and, therefore, on our thinking abilities.
source: "Edsger W. Dijkstra, EWD498"
tags: ["computing", "tools"]
---

It's the parenthetical — "and devious!" — that does the work. The tools
don't just shape what you can do; they shape what you can think *to* do,
which is harder to notice.
