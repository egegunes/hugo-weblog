Hi. I'm **Jane Reader**, and this is my weblog.

I write three kinds of things here, on the theory that they all deserve
permanence and a date, even if they're short:

- **Entries.** Long-form posts. Notebook pages, half-essays, things that
  took me more than half a day to think through.
- **Links.** Short pointers to things I found worth reading. The title
  links out; my commentary is the bit in your own voice underneath.
- **Quotations.** Sentences I want to remember, attributed, with a line
  or two about why they stuck.

There's a mixed feed at the [front page](/), per-stream feeds for each
type (see the section pages), and a [tag index](/tags/) if you want to
follow a specific topic.

You can reach me at jane@example.com.
